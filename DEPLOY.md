# Card EV — Deploy to GitHub Pages

Follow these steps once and your app will be live at a shareable link forever.

---

## Step 1 — Create a GitHub account
Go to https://github.com and sign up for a free account if you don't have one.

---

## Step 2 — Create a new repository
1. Click the **+** icon in the top right corner
2. Click **New repository**
3. Name it exactly: `card-ev`
4. Make sure it is set to **Public**
5. Click **Create repository**

---

## Step 3 — Upload your files
1. On the repository page, click **uploading an existing file**
2. Drag and drop ALL of these files at once:
   - index.html
   - manifest.json
   - sw.js
3. Scroll down and click **Commit changes**

---

## Step 4 — Enable GitHub Pages
1. Click **Settings** (top tab of your repository)
2. Click **Pages** in the left sidebar
3. Under "Source", select **Deploy from a branch**
4. Under "Branch", select **main** and click **Save**
5. Wait about 60 seconds, then refresh the page

You will see a green box that says:
**"Your site is live at https://yourusername.github.io/card-ev"**

---

## Step 5 — Install on your phone
1. Open that URL in **Safari** on your iPhone
2. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**

It will appear on your home screen like a real app. Tap it and it opens fullscreen with no browser UI. Works completely offline.

---

## Sharing with others
Just send them the link: **https://yourusername.github.io/card-ev**

They open it in Safari, add to home screen, done. No App Store needed.

---

## Making updates later
1. Go to your repository on GitHub
2. Click the file you want to update (e.g. index.html)
3. Click the **pencil icon** to edit
4. Make your changes and click **Commit changes**
5. The live site updates automatically within a minute
